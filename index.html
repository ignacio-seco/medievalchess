Welcome to the Human vs Monster chess like Board Game!

In this game you will freely build your army and test your strategy against an oponent.
In each turn you will be able to activate two character of your army, move it and attack oponent's characters.
To begin with, tell us the name of each player and how much points each player will have to build their armies.
Let the best strategy win!

Setting up the game!

What type of game will you people be playing?

Type A game

In a "type A" game every player can activate two units per turn. It is the player that decides what unit to activate.
The speed atribute of the unit does not play any role in this type of game... The players have all the power!

Type B game

In a "type B" game is the speed atribute who automactly activate each unit based on the speed atribute.
That means that the unit with more speed will be activating first than units with less speed
and even, sometimes, can activate twice before a lower speed unit!
This option makes the game more fluid, since it will choose automatically
who is the next character to make a move and, also, make building your army even more strategic
