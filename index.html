Welcome to the Human vs Monster chess like Board Game!

In this game you will freely build your army and test your strategy against an oponent.
In each turn you will be able to activate two character of your army, move it and attack oponent's characters.
To begin with, tell us the name of each player and how much points each player will have to build their armies.
Let the best strategy win!

It is time to build your army !

Select the unit you want to add in your army and click the button "add Army".
If you want to remove the last character you added, just click on "remove army"
The number of units you can have in your army depends on the points it costs.
You can build your army until the sum of the units get the same ammount of the match points.
